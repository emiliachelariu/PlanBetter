PlanBetter

We have all the time in the world, until we don't! :)) if you think you have time for next exam...then think again :)) Hopefully you use our app. So maybe a calendar to help you structure your time better. A timer, to boost morale or lower it. All the good stuff.

The administrator can:
add, edit and delete exams (e.g. mandatory or optional,
for a subject studied in 2nd year, type of exam first/second/third attempt,
written, partially, online for entire study year, for a specific student group);
schedule sessions for exams and reminder timeline;
approve questions for a specific exam (this can be part of the configuration of exam to need or not approval for questions);
filter on subjects, type of exam, period of time;

Students can:
select his/her exams;
attend to a scheduled session;
submit questions and comments for an exam session;
have a view with student timeline: for the current year;
for a specific period of time have options for filtering: per year, per subject ,per period of time;
sort exams by years/subjects;
